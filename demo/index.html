How to Obtain your Season ticket and Bar code

Welcome to Quick Pass web page. We are happy to offer you a simple and quick method for getting your season ticket and Bar code.

Follow these simple steps

  • First, log in to your account or if youâ€˜re a new user register.
  • Complete the registration form with your details and submit it.
  • Upon successful registration, you will be automatically redirected to the "Registration Successful" page.
  • On the "Registration Successful" page, you'll find a link to download your QR-code.
  • Click on the link to download your QR code as a soft copy to your device.
  • To activate your QR code, proceed to the payment page.
  • Choose your preferred payment method and complete the payment process securely.
  • After successful payment, you will receive a payment confirmation message via email/SMS.
  • After payment confirmation, You can visit your profile page you can view all your season ticket details, including the QR code.
  • From your profile page, you can also manage your profile picture and update it as needed.
  • When traveling by bus, simply present your QR code to the bus conductor for scanning.
  • The conductor will scan the QR code, granting you access to travel on your chosen route.
  • Your season ticket will be updated systemically by crossing the calendar date, ensuring seamless access to bus services.
  • If you encounter any issues or have questions about obtaining your QR code, activating your season ticket, or updating your profile picture, our customer support team is here to help.
